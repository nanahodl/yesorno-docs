# Referrals

The Yes/No **Refer & Earn** program rewards you when the people you bring on board trade. You earn a share of the **trading fees** they pay — and so does anyone *they* bring on. Your friends get a better deal too: the more they trade, the lower their fees.

{% hint style="info" %}
There's **no cap** on how much you can earn. As long as your invitees keep trading, your rewards keep accruing.
{% endhint %}

## How It Works

Your referrals form up to **two levels** beneath you:

| Level                | Who they are                                  | You earn                       |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| **Direct (L1)**      | Users you invited yourself                    | **30%** of their trading fees  |
| **Indirect (L2)**    | Users invited by your direct referrals        | **10%** of their trading fees  |

Rewards are calculated on the **fees actually paid** by your invitees, after any fee discount they qualify for. Earnings flow to you automatically — there's nothing to claim.

## Earning a Discount as an Invitee

Anyone who joins through a referral link automatically becomes part of someone's referral tree. As an invitee, you also benefit:

* The more you trade, the **larger your fee discount** becomes
* Discounts apply automatically at trade time — you'll see the reduced fee directly in the order panel
* No code redemption, no opt-in step

## Getting Your Referral Link

To prevent abuse and reward active traders, the ability to create a referral code unlocks once you reach a **lifetime trading volume threshold**.

Until then, you can still:

* Trade normally and accumulate volume
* Track your progress toward unlocking referrals on the **Refer & Earn** page

Once unlocked:

1. Go to the avatar menu in the top-right and choose **Refer & Earn**
2. Pick a referral code (letters and numbers, 3–30 characters)
3. Share your link — `yesorno.trade/?r={your-code}` — with anyone you want to invite

You can copy your link from the dashboard at any time.

## Tracking Your Earnings

The **Refer & Earn** dashboard shows everything in one place:

| Metric              | What it means                                                       |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Sign-ups**        | Total users in your referral tree (L1 + L2)                         |
| **Active Traders**  | Invitees who traded in the last 24 hours                            |
| **Earnings**        | Cumulative rewards you've earned, with a breakdown over time        |

Switch between **7D / 30D / 90D / All-time** ranges to see how your tree is growing. Data refreshes regularly throughout the day.

## Payouts

* Referral rewards are **settled automatically** to your account in **USDC**
* No claim button, no manual step — once an invitee's trade is processed, the reward is in your pipeline
* You can use your earnings immediately for trading or withdraw them like any other USDC balance

## Rules & Eligibility

* You must hold a Yes/No account in good standing — see the [Terms of Use](../resources/terms-of-service.md)
* Self-referrals, fake accounts, wash trading, and any attempt to game the program are **not eligible** and may result in forfeiture of rewards and account action
* Yes/No reserves the right to adjust reward rates, eligibility rules, and the volume threshold; material changes will be announced in advance
* Users in [restricted jurisdictions](../resources/terms-of-service.md) cannot participate

## FAQ

**When do my rewards start counting?**\
The moment a new user signs up through your link and places their first trade.

**Do I keep earning if my invitees stop trading?**\
You earn whenever they trade. If they pause, your rewards pause too — and resume as soon as they trade again.

**Is there a maximum I can earn?**\
No. Earnings scale with your invitees' activity, with no cap.

**Can I have more than one referral code?**\
You manage your codes from the **Refer & Earn** dashboard. Limits, if any, are shown there.

**What happens if a referred user is later removed from the program?**\
Past rewards already settled to your account are yours. Future fees from that user are no longer counted.
